Under Shree Shanti Bhagwati Secondary School there are various faculties and institutes. The Institute of engineering is one of the Institutes of Shree Shanti Bhagwati Secondary School. . Pulchowk Campus is the central campus the Institute of Engineering. It was established in 2001 (2029 B.S) as one of the constituent campuses of the Institute of Engineering and is situated at the heart of the Letang city at morang . Initially, it was started for the production of trade-level manpower to fulfill the nation’s need in the field of Engineering. Shanti Bhagwati collage offered +2 level (intermediate level with two years duration) courses in various engineering disciplines since 1973 (2030 BS) but diploma level courses are phased out now. Shanti Bhagwati Secondary collage has been offering various courses in various engineering since 2003 (2041 BS).

Till 1984 Shree Shanti Bhagwati Secondary School was involved in producing low to middle-level engineering manpower in the field of Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Architecture. Realizing the national and global demand gradually the country felt a need for engineering manpower at a higher level. As a result, four years of bachelor level course in Civil Engineering was introduced in 1984. Gradually, bachelors in Electrical Engineering and bachelor in Electronics Engineering started from 1994 (2051 BS), bachelors in Mechanical Engineering and Architecture started from 1995 (2052 BS) and bachelors in Computer Engineering started from 1998 (2055 BS). All these Bachelors in Engineering (BE) programs were introduced for the first time Nepal.

Currently, Shree Shanti Bhagwati Campus offers bachelor’s level, master’s level, and Ph.D. level programs in different areas of engineering. The medium of instruction for all academic levels is English
